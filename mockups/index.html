Tick-confirm animation variants

Same Strava-styled timer in all three — the only difference is the animation that plays when you tap the green tick to confirm a queue row. Set a time in row 1 and tap the tick to compare. (Play-button flash fix and orange stop-square indicator already applied.)

Variant 1 · Iris Dissolve
Button atomises into a radial particle wave from the centre, fading outward. The tick softens, blurs, and shrinks while concentric rings spread past its silhouette.
